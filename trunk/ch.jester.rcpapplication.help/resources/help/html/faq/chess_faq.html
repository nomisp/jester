Q: Damn, something does not work! I appreciate your work, but please fix it!
A: Report an issue here: Issues

Q: I'am missing important features/system/rankingsystem. What can I do?
A: Open a request on jester homepage or provide your own plugin to jester.

Q: What systems/rankings are supported?
A: jester v1.0 supports roundrobin, eventually ch-dutch, sonnenborn and buchholz

Q: What can I import?
A: jester v1.0 supports importing player data from SBB & FIDE, either file based or directly via their WebPage. Moreover Tournaments from other jester installations can be imported.

Q: The delivered reports are insufficent. Can I change them?
A: jester v1.0 is using jasper reports. Upon request jester exports it's entire data model and the predefined reports into your workingdirectory. The reports can then be redefined with IReport. (You will need some java knowledge).
